FAQ

Things people ask first.

What data leaves my phone?

Only the AI coach messages you choose to send go to our server (so the model can respond). Raw app durations, package names, and behavioral patterns stay on-device. We never sell or share data.

Why $29.99/year and not free?

Because "free" usually means selling your attention back to you. ScreenSense is paid so it stays on your side: no ads, no engagement loops, no dark patterns. The AI coach also has real running costs we pass on at near cost.

Will it actually block apps?

Yes — Pro lets you set hard daily limits per category (Social, Games, Streaming, …) and a Focus Mode that silences notifications on a schedule. We don't break Android's accessibility rules, so blocks are graceful, not aggressive.

iPhone version?

iOS doesn't expose per-app usage to third-party developers, so we're Android-only at launch. We're following Apple's APIs and will ship an iOS version the moment it becomes possible to do this honestly.

Does the AI coach replace a therapist?

No. It's a habit coach trained on your usage data, not a mental-health professional. If you're struggling beyond screen-time, please talk to a real human. The coach will tell you the same.
